# TellerOne - Live without Barriers

**Role**: Mobile Engineer

**Duration: Sept 2022 – Oct 2023**

**Location**: Deleware, United States

## **Project Overview**

TellerOne is a fintech platform that empowers users to manage their finances seamlessly across multiple currencies and geographies. The platform delivers innovative solutions for payments, savings, loans, and financial management, ensuring a barrier-free experience for users worldwide.

---

## **Technological Expertise**

### **Tools and Frameworks**

- **Flutter**: Enabled cross-platform mobile application development.
- **Bloc**: Simplified complex state management for scalable applications.
- **Firebase**: Provided authentication, analytics, and cloud messaging services.
- **Clean Architecture**: Ensured scalability and maintainability of the codebase.
- **CI/CD**: Automated workflows with Codemagic to optimize development cycles.

---

- **Objective**: Deliver a faster and more seamless payment experience.
- **Implementation**: Built a QR-based payment system using Flutter’s camera and networking libraries.

## **Key Features of TellerOne**

### **1. Multi-Currency Support**

- **Overview**: Seamlessly send, receive, and manage money across multiple currencies and countries.
- **Impact**: Simplifies global financial transactions for personal and business users.

### **2. Overdrafts and Loans**

- **Overview**: Provides instant overdrafts and loans with competitive interest rates.
- **Impact**: Offers financial security during emergencies, fostering user confidence.

### **3. Virtual and Physical Cards**

- **Overview**: Enables users to request debit or credit cards for secure and convenient fund management.
- **Impact**: Facilitates global payments, offering flexibility and control.

### **4. Savings in Any Currency**

- **Overview**: Allows users to create currency-specific savings plans to grow their wealth over time.
- **Impact**: Empowers users to achieve financial goals while earning returns.

## **Challenges and Solutions**

### **Challenge 1**: Ensuring secure and reliable payment transactions.

- **Solution**: Integrated advanced encryption protocols and conducted rigorous testing to ensure reliability.

### **Challenge 2**: Reducing account funding issues.

- **Solution**: Designed user-friendly SDKs for Mono Direct Pay, streamlining the funding process and minimizing errors.

- **Enhanced Transaction Speeds**: Improved transaction efficiency by 20%, reducing user wait times.
- **Platform Scale**: Processed over 200,000 transactions totaling $120,000+.

- **Accelerated Deployment**: Achieved faster time-to-market through CI/CD automation.

TellerOne’s Android app has over 2,000 downloads on Google Play Store, while the iOS app boasts over 3,000 downloads on the App Store. These milestones underscore the platform’s growing popularity and its ability to meet user needs effectively.
